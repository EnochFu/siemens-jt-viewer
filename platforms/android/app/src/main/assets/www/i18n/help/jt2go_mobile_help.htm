Getting around JT2Go

The main toolbar

Begin navigating JT2Go by using the main toolbar. Select options from the toolbar to open dialog boxes.

·         When you open a model, if the main toolbar is not displayed, tapin the corner of the viewer.

·         You can dock or float the main toolbar. To move the toolbar when it is floating, drag it to the new location.

Tips for using dialog boxes

·         To close a dialog box, tap  at the top of the dialog box.

Customize your JT2Go display

1.       From the main toolbar, tap .

2.       In the Settings dialog box, you can select:

·         Language

·         Colors of the background, selected objects, and PMI

·         Orthographic or perspective view

·         Toolbar location: docked or floating

Review the components of an assembly structure  

1.       From the main toolbar, tap .

2.       In the Structure dialog box, tap a part to highlight it in the model.

Non-selected parts appear transparent.

You can also highlight parts by tapping them directly on the model. When you do, they are also highlighted in the structure list.

3.       To review the components of subassemblies, double-tap the subassembly.

The subassembly components are listed and displayed.

4.       When viewing a subassembly, to return to the parent assembly, double-tap the assembly name in the Structure dialog box.

5.       To hide and show a part or subassembly, tap the icon next to its name.

Selectively display the parts of a model

1.       From the main toolbar, tap .

2.       Select the parts to view:

·         Show all parts

Show all the models parts.

·         Hide/Show selected

Hide and show the selected parts.

·         Show/Hide PMI for selected

Show and hide the PMI for the selected parts.

·         Open the Properties dialog box

View the properties of a part.
